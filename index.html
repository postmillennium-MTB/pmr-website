About

Rider, advocate, builder.

Post Millennium Renaissance MTB is the work of Jon — a trail advocate, content creator, and tool builder based in Castle Rock, Colorado. The tools here are built without a formal coding background, out of a conviction that mountain bike mechanics and trail economics deserve the same rigor we bring to the trail itself.

The work spans two audiences: technically sophisticated riders who want to understand what's actually happening inside their wheel builds and suspension, and civic stakeholders who need clear, defensible data to invest in trails and open space.

No solutions — only tradeoffs, understood.
