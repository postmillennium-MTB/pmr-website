Uncheck a bike to hide it from the chart (and the fit line) without deleting it. Weight steps 0.1 lb (0.05 kg) per click; price steps $50 (or the CHF equivalent) per click. Adjusted bikes are marked * on the chart and move in real time. “Reset” returns a value to the listed default.

Prices are approximate US MSRP at launch; weights are manufacturer-claimed or magazine-verified for the listed build (typically size M/L, tubeless, no pedals). Treat individual points as ±0.5 lb / ±a few hundred dollars — the pattern is the point, not any single dot. CHF conversion uses the latest available rate, fetched from the European Central Bank via Frankfurter and cached locally for a few days — so it's current within a business day or so, not real-time. Frame material is the main frame construction; some "carbon" bikes use alloy rear triangles. Bikes you add, weight/price tweaks, and checkbox states live only on this page load — refresh clears them.
